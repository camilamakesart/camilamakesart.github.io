About

Camila is a digital and traditional artist with experience in graphic design. She is based out of Nirobi, Kenya and her traditional works are often inspired by this East African context. Camila's design work is often linked to real world issues. In the past she has designed awareness campaigns against single use plastics and consulted with Plastiki Rafiki on packaging and product design. Most recently, Camila has worked to design the show logo for Arc of Justice. Contact her at cparsonsmuniz@gmail.com
